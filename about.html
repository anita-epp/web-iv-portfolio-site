about

Born in SanFrancisco, Anita Epp spent her childhood in the rural ghettos of Northern California. She now resides in the heart of the country - Lincoln, Nebraska - with her husband and four children. She studied Graphic Design at Southeast Community College, and solves creative problems both locally and nationally.

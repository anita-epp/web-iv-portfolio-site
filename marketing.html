poster design for lecture series

These posters were designed for a SanFrancisco lecture series by the world-renown Dr. Lundquist.

The posters reflected the topics covered by Dr. Lundquist on each of the three days of the lectures - Confidence, Hate, and Confusion.

The monochromatic color schemes were chosen to reflect the emotions depicted on each poster.
